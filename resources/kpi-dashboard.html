Analytics Toolkit

Faith Commerce KPI Dashboard

Deploy this Notion + Looker Studio stack to monitor revenue, margin, repeat buyers, and ChatGPT-assisted conversions in real-time.

Dashboard Components

Notion Control Tower

Operational HQ for marketing cadences, supplier tasks, and weekly reviews. Includes board views for launch timeline, asset library, and partner outreach.

Looker Studio Revenue Board

Pulls Shopify, AutoDS, and Klaviyo metrics into a single dashboard. Prebuilt segments for bracelets, devotionals, and bundle orders.

Airtable CRM

Tracks wholesale inquiries, ministry partners, and ChatGPT recommendation performance. Automations forward hot leads to Slack.

Triple Whale Pixel

Attribution + cohort analysis for paid and organic campaigns. Configured for Facebook, TikTok, and ChatGPT store traffic.

Core Metrics

Weekly Snapshot

  • Revenue – total Shopify sales plus manual invoices (goal: $5,000+)
  • Contribution Margin – revenue minus product cost and app spend (goal: 55%+)
  • ChatGPT Assisted Orders – conversions tagged with chatgpt (goal: 30% of orders)
  • New Subscribers – Klaviyo growth by source (goal: 200/week after month 1)

Monthly Health

  • Repeat Purchase Rate – track via Shopify analytics (target: 28%+)
  • Average Order Value – broken out by bundle vs single SKU (target: $65+)
  • Content Velocity – TikTok + Instagram posts published (goal: 60+ pieces)
  • Wholesale Pipeline – number of open ministry / corporate deals (goal: 12 active)

Implementation Steps

  1. Duplicate the Notion template and add your entire team. Link the "Product Releases" database to your Google Drive assets.
  2. Open the Looker Studio template and connect Shopify (via Connector), Klaviyo, and Google Ads. Refresh data daily at 6am.
  3. Install Triple Whale, add your ad platforms, and activate pixel + post-purchase survey.
  4. Sync Airtable with Shopify customers via Zapier so high-value buyers trigger partnership outreach.
